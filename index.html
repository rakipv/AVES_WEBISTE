About School

Amaravani Educational Society is a co-educational school that seeks to provide education of the highest academic standards. Established in 1995, with the aim of providing a well balanced curriculum that emphasizes all-round development of its students, Excellent infrastructure, sound organizational support and ample opportunity to students to develop their potential to the fullest. Provides a superior instructional method in the various disciplines to students of intellectual diversity and varied cultural backgrounds Reach Out, Reach High and Reach Beyond, the motto of the school is reflected in every act of theirs. Amaravani High School & Vani International Public School, Bangalore was established by Amarvani Education Society to provide mass education through innovative methods and also imparting quality education. The trust was farmed in the year 1997 by set of academicians, highly educated professionals, legal experts.
