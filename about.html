Read To Know About Our Key To Excellence

History

Amaravani High School & Vani International Public School, Bangalore was established by Amarvani Education Society to provide mass education through innovative methods and also imparting quality education. The trust was farmed in the year 1997 by set of academicians, highly educated professionals, legal experts.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide a joyful learning environment that empowers children to reach their educational and personal potential while nurturing their self-confidence and self-esteem. Harvest International recognizes that each child is an individual; that all children are creative; that all children need to succeed and believes in the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical.

GOALS:

To provide our students with a conducive learning environment in which a comprehensive scholastic programme which equips them to rise to global standards

To provide an education that is cognizant of the changing demands of our society and develops sensitivity and responsibility.

To provide excellent infrastructure, educational materials & technology to enhance our curriculum

To assist the parent in discovering the potential of their child; to give the student the diagnosis needed to set them towards that goal; and to develop the skills needed to be a winner in life

Philosophy

Nurture a generation of smarter children and encourage independent thinking rather than restricting their creativity to mere syllabus

Share parents responsibility of all-round development of their child.

Encourage children to explore, experiment, discover and learn on their own in guided and mentored environment

To instil confidence, civic sense, good habits, social, cultural and citizenship values

The Vision of the Amaravani Education Society is to fulfill its cherished ambition to spreading out wings of courses in future like Graduation in Legal Studies, Business Management, Commerce & Industry related Degrees and many more. AVES is a co-educational School and offers classes From Nursery to Xth.
